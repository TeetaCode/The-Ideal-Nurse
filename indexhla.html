The Health Literacy Initiative

...awareness for ALL!

The Health Literacy Initiative, is a solution to the Sustainable Development Goal 3, our main goal is to motivate and teach individuals to be intentional about their health, by assisting them to constantly make conscious efforts, in every possible way.

Improved health literacy levels enhance health promotion and disease prevention in families, which leads to a reduction in mortality and morbidity rates, and increased longevity in the community.

Our Services

Community Visits

We assist communities to learn and unlearn concepts that are associated with their health by counselling, teaching and mobilizing them to always make healthy decisions.

Health Literacy courses

The courses are basically for people who wish to understand basic health concepts, and recommended lifestyles, to empower them and assist them to make good health decisions.

Outreach Planning

We help organizations who wish to do community outreaches to plan their events to ensure their goals are met in the long-run.
